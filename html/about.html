Meet the contributors behind Ages Unveiled

Our Mission

Ages Unveiled is an educational group project created to present ancient civilizations through modern web technologies in an engaging and accessible way.

As students at York City College, we collaborated closely, combining historical research with frontend development to ensure both accuracy and a strong user experience.

This project highlights how teamwork and technology can bring history to life for modern audiences.
