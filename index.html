About This Project

Ages Unveiled is a digital journey through time, created to make ancient history accessible and engaging for everyone. We focus on four pivotal civilizations that left an indelible mark on human history.

Each civilization page features a timeline, key facts, and cultural highlights. Our goal is to present history in a clean, modern way without the complexity of frameworks or libraries.
